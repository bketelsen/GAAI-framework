## I. AUTHORITY SIGNAL ARCHITECTURE

### AKU-PA001 — Strategic Differentiation & Narrative Positioning

**Type:** Correlational
**Confidence:** High

Clear, differentiated positioning is associated with:

* Increased professional mobility
* Increased social capital accumulation
* Greater network leverage

**Mechanism:**
Structured identity clarity improves recognition and recall.

**Guardrail:**
Correlation does not establish that branding alone causes career mobility.

**Cross-ref:** Extends WRT-001 AKU-W002 (USP) from content-level to person-level. The author needs a USP just like each content piece does.

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic input for `memory/domains/content-production/voice-guide.md` — the voice guide must define the author's narrative positioning (not just tone). Informs CNT-001-research topic selection: content must reinforce the positioning.

---

### AKU-PA002 — Expertise + Trustworthiness as Measurable Authority Dimensions

**Type:** Causal (Scale Validation) + Correlational (Outcome Linkage)
**Confidence:** High

Authority perception can be operationalized via two primary dimensions:

* Perceived expertise
* Perceived trustworthiness

Validated scales demonstrate positive influence on persuasion and downstream decision-making.

**Optimization Rule:**
Authority must be measurable, not purely aesthetic.

**Cross-ref:** Complements SEO-001 AKU-001 (E-E-A-T) — PA002 applies E-E-A-T to the individual author, not just to content. E-E-A-T is the content signal; expertise + trustworthiness are the person signals.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-009-quality-gate should include author authority signals as a scoring dimension: does the content demonstrate the author's expertise? Does it build trust through transparency?

---

### AKU-PA003 — Heuristic Credibility Cues

**Type:** Observational + Normative
**Confidence:** Moderate

Visible legitimacy markers (credentials, institutional affiliation, transparent disclosure) improve rapid credibility judgments under cognitive constraints.

**Cognitive Mechanism:**
Users rely on heuristics in high-load environments.

**Guardrail:**
Heuristic cues increase perceived trust, not necessarily actual expertise.

**Cross-ref:** Extends SRC-001 CRED002 (Provenance Transparency — Who/How/Why) with the cognitive psychology angle.

**GAAI mapping:** Author page and bio construction. CNT-005-seo-optimize should include author bio/credentials in structured data. CNT-007-social-adapt should include credentials in platform bios.

---

### AKU-PA004 — Topic Focus (Niche Consistency)

**Type:** Large-Scale Correlational (Regression)
**Confidence:** High

Lower topic diversity correlates with:

* Higher retweet impact
* Faster follower accumulation
* Stronger influence signals

**Operational Principle:**
Focused expertise compounds more efficiently than broad generalism.

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic constraint for CNT-001-research topic selection. Content topics must cluster within the defined niche (per voice guide positioning). The backlog should enforce topic consistency — resist the temptation to write about everything.

---

### AKU-PA005 — Publishing Cadence

**Type:** Correlational
**Confidence:** Moderate

Increased posting frequency correlates with:

* Higher engagement
* Incremental follower growth

Diminishing returns appear at very high volumes.

**Guardrail:**
Causality not established; growth may precede cadence.

**Cross-ref:** Related to SOC-001 AKU-SOC005 (Time Decay on X) which covers platform-specific frequency needs.

**GAAI mapping:** Content calendar parameter in `memory/domains/content-production/platform-rules.md`. Cadence should be sustainable given the content pipeline capacity — better to publish consistently at lower frequency than to burn out at high frequency.

## II. ALGORITHMIC AMPLIFICATION PATTERNS

### AKU-PA006 — Two-Stage Ranking Architectures

**Type:** Causal (Platform A/B) + Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High

Modern recommendation systems operate in two phases:

1. Candidate generation (broad retrieval based on profile/topic signals)
2. Ranking optimization (engagement depth scoring)

Optimizing for both stages increases amplification probability.

**Quantified Signals:**
Measured improvements in CTR, sessions, and application conversions.

**GAAI mapping:** Informs CNT-007-social-adapt strategy: Phase 1 (candidate generation) requires topic consistency + entity clarity; Phase 2 (ranking) requires engagement depth (dwell time, interaction). Content must be optimized for both.

---

### AKU-PA007 — Engagement Depth (Watchtime / Dwell Time)

**Type:** Causal (Platform Experiments)
**Confidence:** High

Platforms prioritize engagement depth over superficial views.

Shifting to watchtime optimization may reduce raw views but increase systemic ranking value.

**Optimization Rule:**
Design for sustained attention, not clickbait.

**Cross-ref:** Extends SOC-001 AKU-SOC003 (Interaction Depth Multiplier) with platform experiment evidence and the counter-intuitive insight: fewer views but higher value.

**GAAI mapping:** Reinforces SOC-001 Tier 1 levers (dwell time > likes). CNT-007-social-adapt and CNT-008-youtube-script must prioritize retention over reach metrics.

---

### AKU-PA008 — Conversation Contribution (LinkedIn)

**Type:** Observational + Experimental System Evidence
**Confidence:** Moderate

Content that stimulates professional conversation increases amplification likelihood.

Network interaction density drives feed visibility.

**GAAI mapping:** LinkedIn-specific rule for CNT-007-social-adapt — posts should end with genuine discussion prompts (not "agree?" engagement bait). Content that provokes professional debate outperforms content that provokes reactions.

---

### AKU-PA009 — Fan-Out Topic Coverage

**Type:** Correlational
**Confidence:** Moderate

Coverage across related subtopics ("fan-out queries") correlates with higher AI citation probability.

**Guardrail:**
Explicitly correlational; no proven causal mechanism.

**Cross-ref:** Complements SEO-001 AKU-013 (Topic Cluster Architecture) — clusters are the content structure; fan-out is the personal authority effect of covering a topic cluster comprehensively.

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic input for CNT-001-research — content planning should map the author's topic cluster and systematically cover subtopics. Each new piece strengthens the cluster authority.

### AKU-PA011 — Structured Author Entity Markup

**Scope: dual — `coding/` for implementation + `content/` for author page content**

**Type:** Normative Technical Standard
**Confidence:** High

Implementation of:

* author.url
* sameAs (linking to social profiles)
* ProfilePage schema

Enhances machine recognition of the individual as a distinct entity.

**GAAI mapping:** Schema implementation is `coding/` blueprint. Author page content (bio, credentials, expertise description) is informed by voice guide. CNT-005-seo-optimize checklist should verify author schema presence.

**Confidence:** Moderate

Direct ranking impact remains unquantified.

**GAAI mapping:** Long-term authority building goal. Achieved through consistent entity signals across platforms (PA011), topic focus (PA004), and earned media mentions (SEO-001 AKU-015). Not a content production action but an outcome of sustained content strategy.

**Type:** Observational Survey Evidence

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic context — validates the personal authority approach. In low-institutional-trust environments, individual expert voices carry disproportionate influence. Not actionable as a skill parameter.

### AKU-PA018 — Social Endorsement Cues

**Confidence:** High

## VI. PERSONAL AUTHORITY COMPOUNDING MODEL

### Core Inputs (Weighted by Evidence Strength)

* Topic focus (High confidence, correlational) — PA004
* Expertise + trust signals (High confidence, causal + correlational) — PA002
* Publishing cadence (Moderate confidence) — PA005
* Structured entity markup (High confidence, normative) — PA011

### Amplification Mechanisms

* Engagement depth (High confidence, causal) — PA007
* Conversation contribution (Moderate confidence) — PA008
* Fan-out coverage (Moderate confidence, correlational) — PA009

### Reinforcement Loop (Correlational, Moderate Confidence)

AI citation → Increased CTR → Increased engagement signals → Increased algorithmic amplification

### Compounding Function (Conceptual Model)

Authority ≈ f(Topic Consistency × Trust Signals × Amplification Depth × Entity Clarity)

**GAAI mapping:** This model defines the long-term content strategy objective. Each content piece produced through the blueprint contributes to the compounding function. The voice guide should encode the target position for each input variable.
