## I. SYSTEM-LEVEL ENGAGEMENT DYNAMICS

### AKU-SOC001 — Reach Compression with Conversion Intensification

**Type:** Correlational
**Evidence:** Large-scale creator datasets (1.5M+ posts)
**Confidence:** High

Median reach across major platforms has declined (approx. 24–34%) due to feed saturation.

However, conversion rates are increasing as algorithms prioritize relevance over virality.

**Strategic Implication:**
Optimize for:

* Qualified engagement
* Audience alignment
* Intent matching

Not raw impressions.

**Guardrail:**
Do not equate lower reach with lower ROI.

**Cross-ref:** Aligns with GEO-001 AKU-GEO011 (High-Intent Conversion Amplification) — same shift from volume to qualified exposure, but in social context.

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic context for CNT-007-social-adapt — success metrics should weight engagement quality (shares, saves, DMs) over impressions. CNT-009-quality-gate social scoring must reflect this.

---

### AKU-SOC002 — Share-Dominant Engagement Shift

**Type:** Observational
**Evidence:** 70M+ post analysis
**Confidence:** High

Public comments are declining while private shares (DM forwarding) are rising significantly.

**Algorithmic Insight:**
Private sharing signals high perceived value.

**Optimization Rule:**
Design content worth privately sharing, not just publicly reacting to.

**GAAI mapping:** Core design principle for CNT-007-social-adapt — every social derivative must pass the "would someone DM this to a colleague?" test. Shareability > reactability.

---

### AKU-SOC003 — Interaction Depth Multiplier

**Type:** Algorithm Documentation + Observational
**Confidence:** High

Formats requiring active engagement (e.g., carousels, "see more" expansions) receive algorithmic amplification.

Example:
Native document posts outperform plain text significantly.

**Optimization Pattern:**
Engineer micro-interactions:

* Swipe actions
* Expand clicks
* Multi-slide progression

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt must produce format-appropriate derivatives: carousel slides for LinkedIn/Instagram, thread structure for X, document posts where supported. Not just text reformatting.

---

### AKU-SOC004 — Social SEO Supremacy

**Type:** Observational / Heuristic
**Confidence:** High

Keyword optimization inside:

* Captions
* Subtitles
* Spoken audio

Outperforms hashtag-only strategies.

**Platform Shift:**
Discovery is increasingly semantic, not tag-based.

**Cross-ref:** Mirrors the shift documented in SEO-001 AKU-012 (Topical Coverage vs Keyword Density) and GEO-001 AKU-GEO014 (Keyword Density Devaluation) — semantic intent > literal keywords, now across social too.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt must embed target keywords naturally in captions, not as hashtag lists. CNT-008-youtube-script must optimize spoken keywords in script.

---

### AKU-SOC005 — Time Decay Acceleration (X Model)

**Type:** Algorithm Documentation / Observational
**Confidence:** High

Content visibility on X decays rapidly (approximate half-life within hours).

**Operational Requirement:**
Higher posting frequency required for timeline presence.

**Guardrail:**
Avoid overposting without value density.

**GAAI mapping:** Platform-specific rule for CNT-007-social-adapt — X derivatives need higher volume but can be shorter. LinkedIn derivatives need lower volume but higher depth. Platform-frequency mapping should be encoded in `memory/domains/content-production/platform-rules.md`.

## II. HOOK ENGINEERING FRAMEWORK

### AKU-SOC006 — Pattern Interrupt Superiority

**Type:** Controlled A/B Tests
**Confidence:** High

Hooks that challenge assumptions or contradict expectations outperform neutral summaries.

**Implementation Rule:**
Open with:

* Counterintuitive claim
* Unexpected data
* Industry myth disruption

**Cross-ref:** Complements HMN-001 AKU-AUTH011 (Skimming Effect) — AI text is "too smooth" and easy to skip. Pattern interrupts create the friction that increases engagement.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt must generate 3+ hook variants per post, prioritizing pattern interrupt hooks. CNT-009-quality-gate evaluates hook strength.

---

### AKU-SOC007 — Brevity Premium (Hook Layer)

**Type:** Observational
**Confidence:** Moderate

Hooks under ~12 words show improved performance.

**Mechanism:**
Reduced cognitive load during initial scroll.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt validation rule — first line must be ≤12 words. This is a hard constraint, not a guideline.

---

### AKU-SOC008 — Reverse Storytelling ("Payload First")

**Type:** Experimental + Observational
**Confidence:** High

Deliver the result or quantified outcome immediately (especially within first 2 seconds of video).

**Purpose:**
Mitigate scroll abandonment threshold.

**Cross-ref:** Social-specific application of GEO-001 AKU-GEO005 (Answer-First / BLUF) and WRT-001 AKU-W009 (Inverted Pyramid). Same principle, platform-adapted: result first, story second.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt and CNT-008-youtube-script must front-load the outcome/insight. No "let me tell you a story" openings.

---

### AKU-SOC009 — Reducible Uncertainty Optimization

**Type:** Experimental / Cognitive
**Confidence:** High

Optimal hooks reveal enough to provoke curiosity, but not enough to fully resolve the question.

**Cognitive Mechanism:**
Curiosity gap balanced with clarity.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt hook generation rule — tease the insight, don't give it away. The hook must create a knowledge gap that the rest of the post resolves.

## III. FORMAT-SPECIFIC PERFORMANCE TRENDS

### AKU-SOC010 — Mid-Form Video Dominance

**Type:** Observational (1.1M+ videos)
**Confidence:** High

Videos between 60–90 seconds outperform ultra-short clips in reach.

**Implication:**
Depth within short-form ecosystems is increasingly rewarded.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-008-youtube-script should target 60-90s for short-form derivatives (Shorts, Reels, TikTok). Longer YouTube content follows different rules.

---

### AKU-SOC011 — Elite Long-Form Text Performance

**Type:** Correlational
**Confidence:** High

Top-performing text posts are longer than average but maintain low readability complexity.

**Optimization Rule:**
Combine depth with simplicity.

**Cross-ref:** Reinforces WRT-001 AKU-W013 (Length vs Depth Trade-Off) and HMN-001 AKU-AUTH009 (Readability Paradox). Long-form social works when complexity is low.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt for LinkedIn — allow longer posts (1000+ chars) but enforce low readability score. Depth ≠ complexity.

---

### AKU-SOC012 — Carousel Resilience

**Type:** Observational (35M posts)
**Confidence:** High

Carousels demonstrate stable engagement relative to static image decline.

**Strategic Advantage:**
Sequential micro-commitment.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt should produce carousel versions for LinkedIn and Instagram when content has 3+ distinct points. Blog → carousel is a high-ROI repurposing path.

## IV. REPURPOSING & EFFICIENCY MODEL

### AKU-SOC013 — Pillar-to-Cluster ROI

**Type:** Observational / Case Study
**Confidence:** High

Repurposing one core asset into multiple derivatives significantly reduces production cost and time.

**Strategic Framework:**
1 Pillar → 10–20 micro-assets.

**Cross-ref:** Validates the content blueprint pipeline architecture: one blog post (CNT-003 → CNT-005 → CNT-006) feeds CNT-007-social-adapt which produces multiple platform-specific derivatives. Also aligns with SEO-001 AKU-013 (Topic Cluster Architecture).

**GAAI mapping:** Core justification for CNT-007-social-adapt and CNT-010-repurpose skills. A single backlog story for a blog post should automatically spawn social derivative tasks.

---

### AKU-SOC014 — Visual Memorability Lift

**Type:** Controlled A/B + Cognitive Research
**Confidence:** High

Visual reformats increase memory retention significantly.

**Optimization Rule:**
Convert dense research into:

* Carousels

* Visual breakdowns

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt should flag content with data/statistics as carousel/infographic candidates. Visual production may require external tooling beyond the text pipeline.

**Confidence:** Moderate

Direct calls to engage increase reach marginally.

**Type:** Experimental

External link previews may reduce reach due to platform retention incentives.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-007-social-adapt formatting rule — place links in comments (LinkedIn, X) rather than in the main post body. For blog promotion posts, deliver value in the post first, link in follow-up.

## VII. SAFE SOCIAL OPTIMIZATION PROTOCOL

When generating social strategy through the content blueprint:

1. Classify platform:
   * High decay (X) — frequency + brevity
   * Interaction-depth weighted (LinkedIn) — depth + format variety
   * Watch-time weighted (TikTok) — retention curve optimization
   * Save/share weighted (Instagram) — visual + shareability

2. Optimize hook for:
   * Scroll threshold (SOC007)
   * Curiosity tension (SOC009)
   * Cognitive efficiency (SOC006)

3. Prioritize:
   * Shares (SOC002)
   * Saves (Tier 1 lever)
   * Dwell time (SOC003)

4. Avoid:
   * Over-optimization for vanity metrics (SOC001)
   * Excessive frequency without depth (SOC005)
   * Platform-agnostic posting strategies (SOC004)

**GAAI mapping:** This protocol becomes the decision tree for CNT-007-social-adapt. Platform classification determines formatting rules, hook strategy, and success metrics.
