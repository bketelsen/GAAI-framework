## I. CORE WRITING PRINCIPLES

### AKU-W001 — Writing as Measurable Sales Instrument

**Type:** Normative Strategic Principle
**Confidence:** High
**Stability:** Long-term

Professional writing must be treated as a performance tool designed to drive measurable business outcomes.

**Implication for Model Behavior:**

* Optimize for clarity over ornamentation.
* Optimize for conversion, not aesthetic novelty.
* Frame recommendations in performance terms.

**Guardrail:**
Do not assume all writing contexts are sales contexts (e.g., journalism, academic writing).

**GAAI mapping:** Core principle for CNT-003-draft and CNT-004-edit. Every content piece must have a defined performance objective before drafting begins (set during CNT-002-outline).

---

### AKU-W002 — Unique Selling Proposition (USP)

**Type:** Foundational Persuasion Principle
**Confidence:** High

Every persuasive asset must communicate a specific, unique, and competitive proposition.

**Operationalization:**

* Identify differentiator.
* Express benefit in concrete terms.
* Avoid generic claims ("best", "innovative", etc.).

**Failure Mode to Avoid:**
Vague differentiation framed as uniqueness.

**GAAI mapping:** Input requirement for CNT-002-outline — the outline must define the USP of the content piece. CNT-004-edit should verify USP clarity during editorial pass.

---

### AKU-W003 — Specificity Bias

**Type:** Strong Correlational Principle
**Confidence:** Medium-High

Specific numbers, processes, and concrete details increase persuasion versus vague superlatives.

**Model Instruction:**

* Prefer quantified claims.
* Include mechanism explanation.
* Replace adjectives with evidence.

**Guardrail:**
Never fabricate statistics for persuasive effect.

**Cross-ref:** GEO-001 AKU-GEO006 (Specificity Premium) adds the "Concreteness Bias" mechanism hypothesis. SEO-001 AKU-011 (Quantitative Fact Density) covers the SEO citation angle.

**GAAI mapping:** Execution rule for CNT-003-draft and validation criterion for CNT-004-edit (flag adjectives without evidence).

---

### AKU-W004 — Audience Self-Interest Principle

**Type:** Normative Cognitive Principle
**Confidence:** High

Audiences prioritize their own benefit over brand identity.

**Operational Rule:**

* Emphasize transformation.
* Emphasize benefit over feature.
* Reduce brand-centered narrative.

**GAAI mapping:** Core principle for CNT-003-draft. Cross-validates with AGT-001 AKU-AI016R (Brand-Calibrated Drafting) — voice guide must balance brand voice with reader-centric framing.

---

### AKU-W005 — Test-Measure-Refine (TMR Loop)

**Type:** Performance Optimization Framework
**Confidence:** High

Writing effectiveness must be validated through testing.

**Model Behavior:**

* Suggest A/B testing headlines.
* Set measurable KPIs.
* Avoid declaring copy "optimal" without validation.

**GAAI mapping:** Post-publish feedback loop. Not a content production skill per se — informs iterative improvement of published content. Relevant for content repurposing (CNT-010) when updating based on performance data.

## II. STRUCTURAL PERSUASION MODELS

### AKU-W006 — Awareness Mapping (Schwartz Pyramid)

**Type:** Strategic Audience Calibration
**Confidence:** Medium-High

Match message to awareness stage:

* Unaware → Highlight problem
* Problem Aware → Agitate consequences
* Solution Aware → Differentiate mechanism
* Product Aware → Offer proof
* Most Aware → Offer incentive

**Guardrail:**
Do not assume uniform awareness across segments.

**GAAI mapping:** Input parameter for CNT-002-outline. The outline must declare the target audience awareness stage. CNT-003-draft adapts tone and structure accordingly.

---

### AKU-W007 — Sequential Momentum (AIDA)

**Type:** Classical Persuasion Model
**Confidence:** High

Sequence:

1. Attention
2. Interest
3. Desire
4. Action

Use when emotional escalation is required.

**GAAI mapping:** Structural template option for CNT-002-outline. Best suited for: landing pages, product announcements, bottom-of-funnel content.

---

### AKU-W008 — Pain Amplification Model (PAS)

**Type:** Emotional Trigger Model
**Confidence:** Medium-High

Problem → Agitation → Solution.

Effective for pain-driven markets.

**Guardrail:**
Avoid exaggerated fear tactics in regulated industries.

**GAAI mapping:** Structural template option for CNT-002-outline. Best suited for: problem-aware audiences, pain-driven verticals. See AKU-W006 for awareness stage matching.

---

### AKU-W009 — Inverted Pyramid (Information Front-Loading)

**Type:** Cognitive Efficiency Pattern
**Confidence:** High

Place key information first.

Best suited for:

* News
* SEO
* Skimmable environments

**Cross-ref:** GEO-001 AKU-GEO005 (Answer-First / BLUF Architecture) provides experimental evidence that this pattern increases AI citation probability. SEO-001 AKU-009 (Answer-First Structure) covers the SEO snippet angle.

**GAAI mapping:** Default paragraph structure for CNT-003-draft in informational content. Mandatory for GEO-targeted content (CNT-006-geo-optimize validation).

---

### AKU-W010 — Narrative Identity Model (StoryBrand)

**Type:** Identity-Based Persuasion Framework
**Confidence:** Medium

Customer = Hero
Brand = Guide
Provide plan + CTA.

**Guardrail:**
Do not overuse narrative in short transactional copy.

**GAAI mapping:** Structural template option for CNT-002-outline. Best suited for: brand storytelling, case studies, about pages. Aligns with AGT-001 AKU-AI016R (voice guide should encode hero/guide positioning).

---

### AKU-W011 — Funnel-Specific Models

**Type:** Contextual Framework Selection

* BAB (Before-After-Bridge) → Short-form transformation
* 4Ps (Promise-Picture-Proof-Push) → High-trust, bottom-of-funnel

Model should adapt structure to funnel stage.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-002-outline must declare funnel position. CNT-007-social-adapt uses BAB for social derivatives. CNT-003-draft selects structural model based on funnel stage.

## III. PERFORMANCE SIGNALS & CORRELATIONS

### AKU-W012 — Headline Leverage Effect

**Type:** Strong Historical Correlation
**Confidence:** High

Headline quality disproportionately impacts performance.

**Instruction:**

* Test multiple headline variants.
* Telegraph benefit.
* Avoid ambiguity.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-002-outline must produce 3-5 headline variants. CNT-009-quality-gate evaluates headline quality as a weighted criterion. CNT-007-social-adapt generates platform-specific headline variants.

---

### AKU-W013 — Length vs Depth Trade-Off

**Type:** Contextual Correlation
**Confidence:** Medium

Long-form performs better in:

* Complex decisions
* High-ticket products

Short-form performs better in:

* Social
* Low-friction environments

**Guardrail:**
Do not assume longer always converts better.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-002-outline declares target length based on content type and intent. CNT-007-social-adapt enforces platform-specific length constraints.

---

### AKU-W014 — Trust Premium (Human vs AI Perception)

**Type:** Emerging Correlation
**Confidence:** Medium

Perceived human authorship correlates with higher valuation.

**Model Implication:**

* Emphasize voice authenticity.
* Avoid robotic tone.

**Guardrail:**
Do not claim human writing is inherently superior in all contexts.

**Cross-ref:** AGT-001 AKU-AI007 (AI Slop Detection Threshold — score benchmark for human-quality content).

**GAAI mapping:** Core concern for CNT-004-edit (editorial pass must humanize AI draft) and CNT-009-quality-gate (voice authenticity scoring).

---

### AKU-W015 — Freshness Multiplier

**Type:** Performance Correlation
**Confidence:** Medium

Updated content correlates with improved performance.

**Cross-ref:** GEO-001 AKU-GEO009 (Machine-Readable Freshness Signals) covers the technical implementation (JSON-LD dateModified).

**GAAI mapping:** Informs CNT-010-repurpose skill — content update cadence based on performance decay signals.

### AKU-W017 — Visual Readability Optimization

**Type:** Cognitive Ergonomics
**Confidence:** High

Use:

* Black text on white
* Short paragraphs
* Descriptive subheads

Avoid:

* Dense blocks
* Decorative punctuation in headlines

**Confidence:** Medium-High

## V. CONTRADICTION ZONES (DEBATE HANDLING)

### UZ-W001 — Brevity vs Completeness

Debate:

* Attention economy favors brevity.
* Expensive decisions require exhaustive detail.

**Model Behavior:**
Select depth based on purchase complexity.

**GAAI mapping:** Resolved at CNT-002-outline stage — outline declares depth level based on audience awareness (AKU-W006) and content type (AKU-W013).

---

### UZ-W002 — Inverted Pyramid vs Narrative Flow

Front-loading optimizes clarity.
Narrative sequencing optimizes emotional transportation.

Choose based on objective.

**GAAI mapping:** Resolved at CNT-002-outline stage — outline declares structural model based on content objective (informational → inverted pyramid, emotional → narrative).

---

### UZ-W003 — AI Integration

AI useful for:

* Ideation
* Headline variants

But human oversight correlates with higher perceived trust.

**GAAI mapping:** Validates the mandatory human-in-the-loop checkpoint in the content pipeline (AGT-001 AKU-AI007).

---

### UZ-W004 — Creativity vs Utility

Creative originality is secondary to performance outcomes.

Model must not prioritize novelty over clarity.

**GAAI mapping:** Principle for CNT-004-edit — during editorial pass, prioritize clarity and performance over creative flourishes.
