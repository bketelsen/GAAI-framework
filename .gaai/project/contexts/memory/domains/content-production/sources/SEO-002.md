## I. INFRASTRUCTURE GATE

### AKU-OP001 — Indexability & Crawl Accessibility Gate

**Scope: `coding/` blueprint — context only for content**

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation (Google Search Central)
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Established

Clear crawl access and index eligibility are prerequisite conditions for ranking and inclusion in AI-driven search features.

### Mechanism:

Search systems must be able to fetch, render, and index HTML content. Blocking via `robots.txt`, `noindex`, improper HTTP status codes, or inaccessible resources prevents eligibility.

### Operational Implications:

* Validate HTTP 200 status for target URLs.
* Detect unintended `noindex`.
* Confirm robots.txt allows crawling.
* Ensure important resources are not blocked.
* Validate canonical consistency.

### Guardrails:

* Eligibility does not guarantee ranking.
* Absence of crawl errors does not imply quality.
* Avoid assuming crawl frequency equals ranking strength.

**Cross-ref:** Prerequisite to all SEO-001 and GEO-001 optimizations — none work if content is not indexed.

**GAAI mapping:** Not actionable in content blueprint. Infrastructure concern for `coding/` blueprint. Content skills should be aware: no indexing = no visibility, regardless of content quality.

## II. CONTENT RELEVANCE SIGNALS

### AKU-OP002 — Helpful, Intent-Aligned Content Signal

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Probable

Content designed primarily for users and aligned with search intent improves ranking competitiveness.

### Mechanism:

Search systems evaluate usefulness, originality, and relevance to query intent. Intent mismatch reduces perceived relevance.

### Operational Implications:

* Map content to dominant query intent (informational, transactional, navigational).
* Ensure topical completeness.
* Avoid auto-generated filler.
* Include demonstrable expertise signals where relevant.

### Guardrails:

* No numeric guarantee of ranking.
* Intent alignment is probabilistic, not deterministic.
* Avoid keyword stuffing as a proxy for relevance.

**Cross-ref:** Extends SEO-001 AKU-001 (E-E-A-T) and AKU-002 (Trust Dominance) with the intent alignment dimension. E-E-A-T is about who wrote it; intent alignment is about whether it answers the right question.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-001-research must classify query intent during topic research. CNT-002-outline must structure content to match the dominant intent. CNT-009-quality-gate should verify intent alignment as a scoring dimension.

### AKU-OP003 — Title Element Topical Clarifier

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Probable

Descriptive, unique title elements improve relevance understanding and influence snippet rendering.

### Mechanism:

Search engines use title elements to infer topical focus and generate search result titles.

### Operational Implications:

* One unique `<title>` per URL.
* Include primary topic term naturally.
* Avoid duplication across pages.
* Avoid keyword repetition.

### Guardrails:

* Title presence does not override poor content.
* Length heuristics are non-binding.
* Over-optimization may reduce quality signals.

**GAAI mapping:** Concrete checklist item for CNT-005-seo-optimize: validate title element is unique, descriptive, includes primary topic term. This was a gap — SEO-001 had no title tag guidance.

### AKU-OP004 — Structured Heading Hierarchy (H1 Alignment)

**Type:** Heuristic
**Evidence Source:** Industry Best Practices
**Confidence:** Moderate
**Causality:** Correlated

Clear H1 alignment with page topic improves structural clarity.

### Mechanism:

Headings help systems parse topical hierarchy and contextual relevance.

### Operational Implications:

* Enforce single primary H1.
* Align H1 semantically with title.
* Avoid empty or generic headings.

### Guardrails:

* Multiple H1s are not automatically penalized.
* Heading structure alone does not determine rank.

**Cross-ref:** Complements GEO-001 AKU-GEO007 (Heading Hierarchy as Chunking Markers) — GEO-001 covers the AI extraction angle; OP004 covers the SEO structural clarity angle. Both point to the same practice.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-002-outline must enforce H1 + H2/H3 hierarchy. CNT-005-seo-optimize validates: single H1, H1 aligned with title, no empty headings. CNT-006-geo-optimize validates: headings serve as chunking markers.

### AKU-OP005 — Internal Link Graph Reinforcement

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Established

Crawlable internal links improve discoverability and structural understanding.

### Mechanism:

Search engines follow HTML anchor links to discover pages and infer site architecture.

### Operational Implications:

* Use `<a href>` links.
* Avoid JS-only links for critical navigation.
* Provide descriptive anchor text.
* Ensure key pages receive internal links.

### Guardrails:

* Link volume without relevance offers limited benefit.
* Internal links do not substitute for content quality.

**Cross-ref:** Supports SEO-001 AKU-013 (Topic Cluster Architecture) — clusters are the strategic model; internal links are the implementation mechanism.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-005-seo-optimize checklist: each published piece must include 3-5 relevant internal links with descriptive anchor text. CNT-002-outline should identify internal link targets during outline phase. Supports cluster architecture (SEO-001 AKU-013).

## III. STRUCTURED DATA & SNIPPET CONTROL

### AKU-OP006 — Structured Data Eligibility & Validation

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Established (for rich result eligibility)

Valid structured data increases eligibility for enhanced search features.

### Mechanism:

Search systems parse schema markup to extract entities and structured attributes.

### Operational Implications:

* Use supported schema types.
* Match markup to visible content.
* Validate required properties.
* Avoid hidden or misleading markup.

### Guardrails:

* Structured data does not guarantee rich results.
* Invalid markup may disqualify eligibility.

**Cross-ref:** Extends SEO-001 UZ-001 (Schema Markup Causality — "no proof schema guarantees AI citation") with the positive angle: schema provides eligibility even if not guaranteed. Also extends GEO-001 AKU-GEO008 (Schema as Parsing Facilitator). MTA-001 AKU-PA011 covers author-specific schema.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-005-seo-optimize technical checklist: Article schema, author schema (PA011), FAQ schema (when applicable), datePublished + dateModified. Validation rule: markup must match visible content.

### AKU-OP007 — Snippet & Preview Governance Controls

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Established

Meta directives (`nosnippet`, `max-snippet`, etc.) control snippet and AI preview extraction.

### Mechanism:

Search engines obey meta robots directives affecting preview generation.

### Operational Implications:

* Apply selectively to sensitive content.
* Avoid accidental suppression.
* Audit snippet policies across site.

### Guardrails:

* Restrictive directives reduce visibility.
* Overuse may limit traffic.

**Cross-ref:** Counterpoint to GEO-001's optimization for AI extraction — OP007 provides the control mechanism when content should NOT be extracted. Useful for premium/gated content strategy.

**GAAI mapping:** Strategic awareness for CNT-005-seo-optimize. Default: allow full snippets and AI extraction. Only apply restrictive directives for explicitly gated content (not relevant for blog posts, but relevant for premium content if applicable).

### AKU-OP008 — Meta Description CTR Influence

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Established (No direct ranking effect)

Meta descriptions do not directly influence rankings but affect click-through rate.

### Mechanism:

Search engines may use meta descriptions in snippets; they are not ranking factors.

### Operational Implications:

* Write unique descriptions per page.
* Reflect actual page content.
* Avoid keyword stuffing.

### Guardrails:

* Do not treat as ranking lever.
* Snippet rewriting may occur.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-005-seo-optimize checklist item: unique meta description per published piece (150-160 characters), summarizing the page's value proposition. Not a ranking factor but a CTR lever — write for the human scanning search results.

## IV. AI EXTRACTABILITY

### AKU-OP009 — Textual Availability for AI Extractability

**Type:** Normative
**Evidence Source:** Platform Documentation
**Confidence:** High
**Causality:** Probable

Important information must be in crawlable text form for eligibility in AI search features.

### Mechanism:

Search systems cannot reliably extract key information embedded only in images or multimedia.

### Operational Implications:

* Provide text equivalents for visual content.
* Include structured summaries.
* Use clear section headers.
* Place answers near top of page when appropriate.

### Guardrails:

* Text placement alone does not ensure AI inclusion.
* Avoid excessive formatting intended solely for manipulation.

**Cross-ref:** Extends GEO-001 AKU-GEO015 (Nugget Preference) and GEO-001 AKU-GEO005 (BLUF) with the specific technical requirement: text must be in crawlable HTML, not images/PDFs/canvas. Also supports SRC-001 AKU-CRED009 (Answer Capsule Isolation).

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-005-seo-optimize rule: all key data points, statistics, and answers must be in HTML text (not embedded in images). Alt text must describe visual content. CNT-003-draft rule: when including diagrams or charts, always provide a text summary.

### AKU-OP010 — Keyword & Semantic Term Placement Discipline

**Type:** Observational
**Evidence Source:** Industry Tool Dataset
**Confidence:** Moderate
**Causality:** Correlated

Presence of target and semantically related terms in prominent areas correlates with relevance signals.

### Mechanism:

Semantic coverage supports entity recognition and topic modeling.

### Operational Implications:

* Include primary topic term in title and body.
* Expand semantic term coverage.
* Monitor repetition thresholds.

### Guardrails:

* Avoid keyword stuffing.
* Correlation does not imply direct ranking cause.

**Cross-ref:** Operational complement to SEO-001 AKU-012 (Topical Coverage vs Keyword Density — the strategic principle) and GEO-001 AKU-GEO014 (Entity Alignment over lexical repetition). OP010 is the execution-level discipline; AKU-012 is the architectural principle.

**GAAI mapping:** CNT-005-seo-optimize validation: primary keyword appears in title, H1, first paragraph, and naturally throughout body. Semantic variants are used (not keyword repetition). CNT-002-outline should identify primary + semantic terms during outline phase.
