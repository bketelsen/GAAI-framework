# Slack & Discord Engagement Messages — E01S01 Listening Phase

> Purpose: Gentle community engagement to surface expert and prospect pain language.
> Rule: Authentic, no pitch, no CTA, no link. Participate as a member, not a marketer.
> Usage: Pick the right channel, drop the message, observe and log responses.

---

## Message 1 — Expert side
**Where:** Freelance/consultant Slack or Discord workspaces
**Target channels:** `#freelance`, `#business`, `#clients`, `#lead-gen`, `#consulting`
**Suggested communities:** Superpath, Indie Hackers Discord, AI Tinkerers, freelance-focused Slack groups

```
Hey — doing some research on lead gen for AI consultants and integrators.

Quick honest question: what % of the leads/inquiries you receive are actually qualified — right budget, decision-maker at the table, clear project scope?

Trying to understand if this is a widespread frustration or just a few people I've spoken to. Happy to share what I find if there's interest.
```

---

## Message 2 — Prospect side
**Where:** Entrepreneur / business owner / startup Slack or Discord
**Target channels:** `#tools`, `#ask-the-community`, `#resources`, `#automation`
**Suggested communities:** Indie Hackers Discord, Product Hunt Makers, startup-focused Slack groups, no-code communities

```
Question for anyone who's hired or tried to hire an AI automation expert:

What was the hardest part? Finding the right person, vetting them, or getting them to understand your specific context?

Curious because I keep hearing very different answers depending on company size.
```

---

## Message 3 — Mixed / AI tool communities
**Where:** AI-focused Discord servers (AI Breakfast, Latent Space, AI Tinkerers, etc.)
**Target channels:** `#general`, `#tools`, `#jobs-and-hiring`, `#freelance`

```
Has anyone here used a platform specifically for finding AI integration consultants — not generic freelance platforms like Upwork, something more specialized?

If yes, what was your experience? If no — how do you currently find specialists when you need one?
```
