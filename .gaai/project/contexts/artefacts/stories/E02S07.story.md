# Story E02S07: Billing & Subscription Page

## User Story

**As a** registered expert,
**I want** to see my credit balance, activate my subscription, review transaction history, and control my spending limits,
**so that** I have full transparency over what I'm paying and can control my exposure.

---

## Context

Billing transparency is a core platform value. This page surfaces all billing data from `GET /api/experts/:id/billing` (E06S38) and provides controls for `max_lead_price` and `spending_limit` — the expert's two levers to control their financial exposure.

**Billing model (DEC-68):** Lemon Squeezy usage-based subscription ($0/month base, carte on file). Credit deduction at lead creation. Flag/restore within 7 days. Usage reported to LS after flag window. 100€ welcome credit.

**Expert controls (DEC-67/68):** `max_lead_price` = maximum price per individual lead (deal-breaker at matching — leads above this price are never delivered). `spending_limit` = maximum monthly spend (leads paused when reached).

## Notes

- **Lemon.js overlay:** LS provides a JavaScript snippet that opens a checkout overlay without redirecting the user. This is the preferred activation flow — expert stays on the dashboard. The LS product must be created with usage-based pricing (see E06S33 SETUP.md).
- **Centimes to EUR:** All amounts in the database are stored in centimes (integer). The dashboard converts to EUR for display (divide by 100, format with 2 decimal places). Input fields accept EUR, convert to centimes before sending to API.
- **Billing transparency (platform value):** The pricing grid (49€–263€) should be visible somewhere on this page — experts should always know the pricing model. A "Comment sont calculés les prix ?" expandable section with the DEC-67 grid is recommended.
- **"Leads manqués" section (AC10):** This is a conversion tool — showing the expert what they're missing encourages them to increase their limits or add credit. But it should never feel manipulative. Framing: "Voici les leads que votre configuration actuelle a filtrés" — factual, not "You're missing out!"
- **PostHog events (Delivery must include):** `expert.billing_viewed` (page load), `expert.subscription_activated` (ls_subscription_id), `expert.spending_control_updated` (control_name, old_value, new_value). Server-side capture in actions.
