# Story E06S22: Semantic Scoring Integration

## User Story

**As a** prospect,
**I want** the matching engine to understand semantic relationships between skills and industries,
**so that** I receive relevant expert matches even when my terminology differs from the expert's profile.

---

## Context

With Vectorize infrastructure in place (E06S21), this story integrates semantic similarity into the matching pipeline. Instead of scoring ALL experts, the pipeline first queries Vectorize for the top-K semantic candidates, then runs deterministic `scoreMatch()` only on those candidates.

Skill and industry scoring are augmented with a vector similarity component: `0.7 × exactMatch + 0.3 × vectorSimilarity`. The manual SKILL_ALIASES and INDUSTRY_PROXIMITY remain as fallback.

---

- [ ] AC7: **Fallback:** if `EXPERT_INDEX` undefined or error → score ALL with deterministic-only path

## Notes

The `semantic_similarity` score in `ScoreBreakdown` is the raw cosine similarity from Vectorize (0.0–1.0). It is used to augment skill and industry scores but is also stored for observability.

Graceful fallback ensures the platform works without Vectorize during development, testing, or if the binding is temporarily unavailable.
