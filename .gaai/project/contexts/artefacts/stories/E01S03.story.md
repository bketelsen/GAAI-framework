# Story E01S03: Prospect-Side Hand Raiser Campaign

## User Story

**As a** founder validating prospect-side demand,
**I want** companies seeking AI automation to publicly raise their hand in response to a targeted post,
**so that** I have evidence that qualified prospects exist and can be reached through social channels.

---

## Context

Prospects are the product delivered to experts. Without confirmed prospect demand, the matching engine has no input. The prospect-side Hand Raiser validates that real companies with AI automation needs will self-identify publicly and engage with the platform concept.

Promise template (adapt language from E01S01 findings):
> "Si je pouvais vous mettre en contact avec 2–3 experts IA pré-sélectionnés selon votre budget et votre secteur, en moins de 48h — est-ce que ça vous intéresserait ? Commentez 'moi' ci-dessous."

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AC1: 50–100 business owners / decision-makers added as connections on LinkedIn before posting
- [ ] AC2: Hand Raiser post published on LinkedIn using prospect-side promise language from E01S01
- [ ] AC3: Same post (adapted) published on X/Twitter
- [ ] AC4: Same post published in at least 2 relevant Facebook Groups (entrepreneurs, business owners, startup founders)
- [ ] AC5: All commenters logged (name, role, company, platform, described need if mentioned) within 48h
- [ ] AC6: Gate assessed: ≥15 qualified prospects raise their hand across all platforms combined

---

## Out of Scope

- Sending prospects to a form or landing page at this stage
- Any product demo or pitch
- Paid promotion

---

## Dependencies

- E01S01 (promise language)
- E01S02 can run in parallel — expert and prospect campaigns can overlap

---

## Notes

"Qualified prospect" = business owner, founder, or manager at a company (not a student, not an agency promoting their own services). Gate is 15 across all platforms combined.

If gate fails (< 15 raisers): rewrite the promise, adjust targeting, repost. Do not proceed to E01S04 until gate is hit.
