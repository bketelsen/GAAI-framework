# Story E01S04: Validate M0 Gates and Lock Promise Language

## User Story

**As a** founder completing Milestone 0,
**I want** to formally assess both gates and document the validated promise language,
**so that** Epic E01 is officially complete and Milestone 1 (build) can begin with confidence.

---

## Context

This story is the formal close of Epic E01. It consolidates the Hand Raiser results, confirms both gates are met, and produces the locked promise language that will be used across the product (landing page copy, form intro, expert onboarding messaging, etc.).

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AC1: Expert gate confirmed: ≥10 qualified experts raised their hand (with evidence log from E01S02)
- [ ] AC2: Prospect gate confirmed: ≥15 qualified prospects raised their hand (with evidence log from E01S03)
- [ ] AC3: Final validated promise — expert version — written in one sentence and approved
- [ ] AC4: Final validated promise — prospect version — written in one sentence and approved
- [ ] AC5: Decision recorded in `decisions/_log.md`: which promise language was validated, on which platforms, with which response counts
- [ ] AC6: Memory updated: `project/context.md` updated with validated promise language
- [ ] AC7: Epic E01 marked complete; E02 and E03 Stories unblocked for Delivery planning

---

## Out of Scope

- Beginning any technical build (that is E02 onwards)
- Contacting hand raisers (that comes in a later phase)

---

## Dependencies

- E01S02 gate passed
- E01S03 gate passed

---

## Notes

If either gate has not been met by Day 7: do not mark this story done. Return to E01S02 or E01S03, refine the promise, and retry. Maximum 2 retries before escalating to Discovery for strategic pivot decision.
