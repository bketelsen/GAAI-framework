# Epic E01: Offer Validated Before Building

## Purpose

The founder has confirmed that both sides of the marketplace (experts and prospects) genuinely want this product — before any development investment is made. This eliminates the risk of building something nobody pays for.

---

## Scope

- Define a sharp, measurable promise for each side of the marketplace
- Execute Hand Raiser validation campaigns on LinkedIn, X/Twitter, and Facebook Groups
- Use Reddit + Slack/Discord for pre-step listening and language sharpening
- Count public hand raisers against gates (≥10 experts, ≥15 prospects)
- Document validated promise language for use in product copy

---

## Out of Scope

- Building any product or technical infrastructure
- Video closing or sales sequences
- Paid advertising
- CRM or automation tooling

---

## Stories

- E01S01: Reddit & community listening to sharpen promise language
- E01S02: Expert-side Hand Raiser campaign (LinkedIn + X + Facebook)
- E01S03: Prospect-side Hand Raiser campaign (LinkedIn + X + Facebook)
- E01S04: Validate gates and lock promise language

---

## Success Metrics

- ≥10 AI integration experts publicly raise their hand
- ≥15 prospects (companies/business owners) publicly raise their hand
- Both gates hit by Day 7
- Promise language confirmed and documented

---

## Dependencies

None — this is the first milestone and requires no prior work.
