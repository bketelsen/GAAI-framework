## Privacy Policy Coverage

The Privacy Policy covers all content required by Google's OAuth verification:
- Data collected: email, Google Calendar events (calendar.events scope)
- Purpose: availability display + booking event creation + Google Meet links
- Data sharing: sub-processors only (Supabase, Cloudflare, Resend, OpenAI, PostHog EU)
- Data retention: until account/OAuth disconnection/deletion
- Google API Services User Data Policy compliance statement (Limited Use)
- User rights: disconnect OAuth, delete account, GDPR access/rectification/portability

## Routing Behaviour

`/privacy` and `/terms` are registered as Hono routes **before** the `app.use('*', ...)` config-resolution middleware. This means:
- Requests to `/privacy` and `/terms` on ANY hostname are served immediately
- No satellite config lookup required
- No risk of 302 redirect loop for `callibrate.io`
- Cache-Control: `public, max-age=86400` (24h CF edge cache)
