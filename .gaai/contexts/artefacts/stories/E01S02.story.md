# Story E01S02: Expert-Side Hand Raiser Campaign

## User Story

**As a** founder validating expert-side demand,
**I want** AI integration experts to publicly raise their hand in response to a targeted post,
**so that** I have unbiased evidence that experts will pay for qualified leads before building anything.

---

## Context

The expert side is the paying customer. Validating their willingness to engage (and implicitly pay) before building is the highest-priority risk to eliminate. A public Hand Raiser post generates honest signal — people either respond or they don't, with no social pressure to be polite.

**Channel constraint (DEC-100):** LinkedIn blocked (founder employed at key position). X/Twitter is primary channel. Discord (n8n) and Reddit are secondary warm-engagement channels. Facebook Groups replaced by X Communities + Skool.

**Promise language (DEC-102):**
- Expert: "Stop filtering tire kickers. Get matched only with prospects who have confirmed budgets, real timelines, and a problem worth solving."
- Validated vocabulary: "tire kickers", "decent leads", "found time", "confirmed budget"

---

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] AC1: ≥2 weeks of warm engagement on X + n8n Discord before posting hand raiser (seed phase — DEC-100 Phase 1-2)
- [ ] AC2: Hand Raiser post published on X/Twitter using promise language from E01S01 AC4 (DEC-102)
- [ ] AC3: Same post (adapted) published in at least 2 communities: n8n Discord (#general or #idea-collaboration) + 1 X Community or Reddit sub
- [ ] AC4: All commenters/repliers logged (name, profile, platform) within 48h of each post going live
- [ ] AC5: Gate assessed: ≥10 qualified experts raise their hand across all platforms combined
- [ ] AC6: LinkedIn hand raiser deferred — to be activated when professional constraint lifts (DEC-100)

---

## Out of Scope

- Responding to hand raisers with a sales pitch or video (not at this stage)
- Paid promotion of posts (reassess if organic < 5 replies after 7 days)
- DM outreach before posting
- LinkedIn activity (professional constraint — DEC-100)

---

## Dependencies

- ~~E01S01~~ (DONE — promise language validated, AC4 delivered as DEC-102)

---

## Notes

"Qualified expert" = profile shows they are an active AI consultant, integrator, or automation freelance. Not a prospect, not a student. Gate is 10 across all platforms combined — not per platform.

If gate fails (< 10 raisers): rewrite the promise and repost before moving to E01S03.

**Warm engagement prerequisite (DEC-100):** Do NOT post hand raiser cold. Minimum 2 weeks of authentic engagement (replying to threads, sharing insights, answering questions) on target channels before posting. This builds credibility and ensures the post reaches warm connections, not strangers.
