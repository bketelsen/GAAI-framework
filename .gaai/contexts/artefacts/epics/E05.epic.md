# Epic E05: Expert Pays for Lead Access and Tracks ROI

## Purpose

An expert is billed automatically via internal credit deduction when a qualified lead is confirmed (7-day flag window), can evaluate the lead quality after the call, declare conversion, and track their full pipeline and ROI — so the platform generates revenue, improves over time, and experts can measure the value they receive.

> **Layer constraint:** This epic delivers Layer 3.1 UI (app.callibrate.io). The Layer 2 billing backend has been split into E06S31–E06S34 (credit schema, consumer revision, LS subscription, matching enforcement). E05 consumes those APIs and renders the expert-facing dashboard and forms.
> **Layer 2 foundation (E06):** E06S31 (schema + pricing), E06S32 (credit debit + flag/restore), E06S33 (LS subscription + usage reporting cron), E06S34 (balance/limit enforcement at matching). These must be delivered before E05 stories.
> **Billing model (DEC-68):** LS usage-based subscription ($0/mois, carte on file). Credit deduction at booking time. Flag/restore within 7 days. Usage reporté à LS après flag window.
> **Pricing model (DEC-67):** Dynamic — budget tier × qualification modifier. Grille publiée. Expert contrôle via max_lead_price + spending_limit.

---

## Scope

- **Dynamic lead pricing (DEC-67):** Budget tier × qualification modifier. Grille publiée : 49€–263€. Prix affiché dans le dashboard avec breakdown (budget tier + qualification level).
- **Credit billing (DEC-68):** LS usage-based subscription ($0/mois, carte on file). Credit deduction interne instantanée à la création du lead. Flag/restore dans les 7 jours. Usage reporté à LS après flag window. 100€ crédit d'accueil.
- **Expert controls:** `max_lead_price` (plafond par lead, deal-breaker au matching) + `spending_limit` (plafond mensuel). Leads pausés si dépassé.
- **Dashboard transparency:** Solde crédits temps réel. Leads manqués (cause : solde insuffisant OU max_lead_price dépassé). qualification_rate visible avec explication d'impact. Spending limit progression.
- **Anti-gaming transparency (DEC-30 + DEC-68):** Règles communiquées dès l'onboarding. qualification_rate affichée dans le dashboard. Framing positif : "confirme tes leads → meilleurs matchs."
- **Expert lead dashboard:** Leads received, calls booked, conversion tracking, composite score visibility
- **Lead evaluation (J+48):** Expert scores lead quality (1–10) and optionally declares conversion — feeds the matching engine feedback loop
- **Conversion declaration:** Expert voluntarily reports if the project started/closed — contributes to `hire_rate` component of composite score
- **Premium options (post-MVP):** Visibility boost, Top Match badge

---

## Out of Scope

- Subscription plans / volume bundles (deferred to post-M3)
- Invoice generation beyond Lemon Squeezy receipts (LS generates receipts automatically as MoR)
- Multi-currency (EUR only at MVP)
- Manual refund/dispute workflow (flag/restore automatique via crédits internes — disputes complexes manuelles uniquement)
- Prospect-side payment (prospects use free)
- ~~Automatic stored-payment-method billing~~ — **Resolved by DEC-68:** LS usage-based subscription provides card-on-file auto-billing
- Auto-top-up instantané (LS ne supporte pas le charge-on-demand — auto-charge fin de cycle uniquement, Lemon.js overlay pour top-up manuel si besoin)
- Credit expiration (pas d'expiration au MVP — réévaluer post-M3)
