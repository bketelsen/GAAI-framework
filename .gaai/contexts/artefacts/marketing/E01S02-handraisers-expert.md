**Accounts to follow and engage with (reply to their threads):**
- n8n (@n8n_io) — reply to workflow examples, builder announcements

- Nick (@heyiamnick) — automation consultant threads (real pain language source)
- Cole Abrams / Make.com builders — workflow automation threads

**What to reply:**

**Frequency:** 3-5 substantive replies per day (not likes — replies). 15-20 min/day.

**Channels to engage in:**
- `#general` — answer tool questions, share tips

**Frequency:** 1-2 genuine contributions per day in Discord. 10 min/day.

### X Communities to join + warm

Find via: x.com/i/communities → search "n8n" / "AI automation"

By end of week 2: recognizable to at least 5-10 accounts in your target community. You have engaged authentically with a mix of technical and business threads. You are not a stranger.
