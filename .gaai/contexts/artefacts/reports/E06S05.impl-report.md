## Notable Decisions

- **ExpertProfile as matching view model**: Rate_min/rate_max folded into ExpertProfile for the pure function (not in profile JSONB). The function's ExpertProfile represents all expert data needed for scoring — mapping happens in the route handler before calling scoreMatch.
- **Timeline match behavior**: When prospect specifies a timeline but expert has no `accepted_timelines`, returns 0 (not full). This incentivizes experts to fill their timeline preferences and avoids false positives.
- **DEC-33 cleanup**: `MATCHING_JOBS: Queue` removed from `env.ts` — was a leftover from E06S02, queue deleted per DEC-33.
